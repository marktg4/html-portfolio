Mark's Favorite Movies

My opinion (as of right now)

Coach Carter

Shoe-in for my favorite sports movie. I grew up with it and I think it's easy to get behind the coach's beliefs despite the adversity.

Lord of the Rings

My nerdy pick of choice, and I can't even decide which of the original 3 I like the best. Huge childhood nostalgia, great action, great soundtrack, and still holds up today.

Shutter Island

A more recent favorite. I love the mystery of it. You are constantly thinking about what's up with the island as the movie progresses, and after the ending, you want to rewatch it again.
